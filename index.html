Parameters

  • speed = 0.866 c
  • uncontracted rocket length = 51.86 light-minutes

Notes

In the "green" rest frame:

  • The green rocket is stationary
  • The red rocket is moving to the right at speed 0.866c.
  • The clocks of the "green" rocket are synchronized.
  • The clocks of the "red" rocket are out-of-synch.
  • The clocks of the "red" rocket run at one-half the rate of those of the "green" rocket.
  • The front clock (the right clock) of the "red" rocket is 45 minutes behind that of the rear clock (the left clock).
  • The length of the "red" rocket is one-half that of the "green" rocket.

In the "red" rest frame:

  • The red rocket is stationary.
  • The green rocket is moving to the left at speed 0.866c
  • The clocks of the "red" rocket are synchronized.
  • The clocks of the "green" rocket are out-of-synch.
  • The clocks of the "green" rocket run at one-half the rate of those of the "red" rocket.
  • The front clock (the left clock, since it's moving to the left) of the "green" rocket is 45 minutes ahead of that of the rear clock.
  • The length of the "green" rocket is one-half that of the "red" rocket.

Formulas

  • "gamma factor" = 1/sqrt(1-v^2/c^2)
  • length of moving rocket contracted by a factor of gamma
  • rate of moving clock slows by a factor of gamma
  • offset of clocks on board moving rocket = gamma L (L = distance between clocks, with the "front" clock running behind)
